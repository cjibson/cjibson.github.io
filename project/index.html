What is Fantasy Football?

  • Fantasy football is a virtual game where participants act as team owners, selecting real-life NFL players to create their own fantasy teams. These teams compete against each other based on the statistical performance of the players in actual NFL games. Owners draft their teams, manage lineups, make trades, and strategize to accumulate points based on various statistics such as touchdowns, yards gained, and receptions. It's a game of skill, strategy, and passion, bringing fans closer to the action and creating a community of sports enthusiasts united by their love for football.

    • Additional rules for my rankings:

  • Superflex: Allows you to start a quarterback in the flex position. This makes quarterbacks more valuable.
  • Dynasty: Long-term fantasy football league where you keep players from year to year. In dynasty formats, younger players are valued higher.
