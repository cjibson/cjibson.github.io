QB Rankings

Lamar Jackson

Lamar Jackson is my QB 1. In 2023, in his first season under new offensive coordinator Todd Monken, Lamar won his second MVP. I expect Lamar to have an even better this upcoming season, and he has the rushing upside to finish as the top QB in 2024.

Josh Allen

Josh Allen finished 2023 as the QB 1 in fantasy football. Although he will be losing his top target in Stefon Diggs, no quarterback in the NFL can put the team on his back like Allen. Due to his willingness to run the ball in the redzone, I expect him to finish as the QB 2 this year.

Patrick Mahomes

Patrick Mahomes had a down year in 2023, but he still finished as the QB 8. I expect him to bounce back and have a better year in 2024. WR Rashee Rice should take another step forward, TE Travis Kelce hopefully won't spend as much time injured, and the Chiefs will most likely add another WR in the draft.

C.J. Stroud

C.J. Stroud took the NFL by storm in 2023. The QB had a fantastic rookie season in which he finished in the top 10 QBs by fantasy points. With an already excellent supporting cast that also added WR Stefon Diggs and RB Joe Mixon, I expect Stroud to be even better this year and finish as the QB 4.

Jalen Hurts

Jalen Hurts has been won of the top QBs in fantasy football over the past few years. Although the former Alabama QB finished as the QB 2 last year, I expect him to take a step back this season due to the retirement of center Jason Kelce. Many of Hurts' touchdowns this last season came on QB sneaks, but without Kelce pushing the pile, I expect his total rushing touchdowns to regress.

Brock Purdy

Going into his 3rd year, former Mr. Irrelevant, Brock Purdy, has had to prove his doubters wrong week in and week out. Purdy finished as the QB 7 in 2023, and he is just too consistent for me to rank him any lower than QB 6. Although he doesn't have rushing upside, he plays on a great team with a great head coach.

Joe Burrow

After a disappointing 2023 in which Joe Burrow spent the last half of the season on injured reserve, I expect the former LSU QB to bounceback. Burrow has a fantastic supporting cast, and he is so good at buying time for his elite WRs to get open down the field.
